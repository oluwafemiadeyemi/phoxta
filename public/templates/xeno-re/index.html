Our Story

Where passion meets the plate

Founded by Chef Antoine Moreau, Saveur is a celebration of seasonal ingredients, classical techniques, and modern creativity. Every dish tells a story — of the land it comes from, the hands that crafted it, and the joy it brings to the table.
